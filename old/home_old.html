Frequently asked questions

Everything you need to know about the service.

How do I find my filter size?

Your current filter’s size is usually printed on the frame (e.g., 16×25×1). If not, measure the length, width, and thickness.

Can I pause or reschedule deliveries?

Yes — you can pause, skip, or adjust the frequency anytime from your account page.

What if I pick the wrong size?

We’ll help you swap for the correct size at no extra cost on your first order.

Where do you ship?

We ship nationwide. Shipping is free for active subscriptions.
